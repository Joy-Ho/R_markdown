Transform

The variable Income is the distribution of right skewness. I use square root transformation to reduce right skewness. Then, I use Box-Cox Transformation to transform data into a normal distribution.

The distribution of Overall Life is left skewed. I use square transformation to reduce left skewness. Then, I use Box-Cox Transformation to convert data into a normal distribution.
